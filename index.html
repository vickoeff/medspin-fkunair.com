MEDSPIN

MEDSPIN is an international Medical Science and Application Competition organized by the students of the Faculty of Medicine, Universitas Airlangga. This year's event will be organized by the class of 2022 & 2023 and will be held both offline and online.

PARTICIPANT'S REQUIREMENTS

  1. Participants are High School students or equivalent, both public and private schools from all over Indonesia and South East Asia that is shown through Student Identity Card
  2. Participants may register more than 1 competition (olympiad, public poster, scientific paper)
  3. For participants that are qualify for olympiad’s quarter final or public poster’s final or scientific paper’s final, they must choose 1 competition and confirm it to the contact person of the competition chosen
  4. Participants are in team of 2-3 people (for public poster and scientific paper) and 3 people (for olympiad) from the same school
  5. There will be a maximum quota for participants, the committees will announce if the number of participants already reach the maximum quota
